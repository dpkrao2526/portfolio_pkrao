Project Overview

Introducing our groundbreaking e-commerce platform: a true revolution in online shopping. By harnessing the power of cutting-edge technologies, we're redefining the shopping experience. Our platform employs sophisticated data gathering techniques, utilizing web scraping to collect information from a wide array of e-commerce sources. But that's just the beginning. We go a step further by subjecting this data to meticulous preprocessing, ensuring its quality and accuracy. Enter advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP). Our system doesn't just stop at product information; it delves into the realm of customer reviews. Through NLP, we're able to decipher the sentiments hidden within these reviews, categorizing them as good, bad, or neutral. This not only provides insightful sentiments but also empowers our users with the knowledge to make informed decisions. But the innovation doesn't halt there.

Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) take center stage, enhancing product categorization. Searching and navigating through our platform becomes a breeze as these networks accurately classify and categorize products, streamlining the user experience. And let's talk personalization. Our platform employs Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) technology to deeply analyze user interactions. This enables us to provide tailored product suggestions that match each user's preferences and interests. It's not just shopping; it's a curated journey that keeps users engaged and satisfied.
